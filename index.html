About me

Ambitious, yet humble - that’s the best way to describe me!
After moving to the U.S. from Brazil, I decided to sharpen my skills and immerse myself into the tech world.
This path led me to an amazing internship at Datadog, where I discovered my passion for Product: from concept, to design, to build, then to launch!
Now, I work at Contentsquare, helping achieve digital experience analytics in a more human way.

Attention to detail, organization, and an eye for good design - those are some of the qualities my teammates like to attribute to my work.
I like to merge creative, analytic and strategic thinking to all my projects,
and I go above and beyond to take things to completion.

On a more personal note, I find relaxation and peace in the kitchen - where I can be creative with my cooking!
